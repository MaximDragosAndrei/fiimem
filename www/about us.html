Why us?

If you want an application that can help you store memories and share with others without overfilling your closets or your computer than you should definitely choose our application.

How it works?

When you first come into our website you must register yourself. After that you will be redirected to our login page. There you have to complete the form with your personal email adress and a password.

After you login it will appear your profile page which contains your username and your photo.

If you will look in the menu bar you will see that you have 3 buttons: Documents, Photos, Videos. By click on one of them you will be able to upload your files depending on what you choose.

Congrats! You are now ready to use our web application. Have fun!
